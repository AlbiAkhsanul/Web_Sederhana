Shaw From Arknight
by : Albi Akhsanul Hakim

Shaw is a character from mobile gacha game called Arknight. From WIkipedia " Arknights is a free-to-play tactical RPG/tower defense mobile game developed by Chinese developer Hypergryph. It was released in China on 1 May 2019, in other countries on 16 January 2020 and in Taiwan on 29 June 2020."


Shaw is well known for her very good Crowd Control (CC) skill, decent stats and the fact that you can obtain her for free after clearing a certain level from the main story. Even thought Shaw is only a four star unit, lots of people still use her in high level due to how versatile she is, need a decent pusher? use Shaw, need someone to kill a horde of small enemies? use Shaw, also teh fact that you can put her in both ground and sniper tiles boost her compability even further.


Other than her combat compability, our little squirel is also known due to how she rap, yes she talks so fast that it literally sounds like she is rapping. Shaw is a firefighter, so her personality is somehow based on that, her lines talk about firefighting stuff and her weapons are firefighter tools, like she uses axe in her "basic attacks" and use the firefighter hose in her skills to push enemy away.
